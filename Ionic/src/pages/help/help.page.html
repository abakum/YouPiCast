Help

 This application aims at connecting your local Raspberry Pi and Youtube application on Android phone.

Let's cover some important sections -

IP Configuration: Make sure you obtain the IP and port of your Raspberry Pi. By default, the IP is set to 192.168.0.115:5000. You can set a stable IP for your Pi by changing the DHCP settings on your network router. Click 'SAVE' to persist your changes.

History: Lets you check the videos you casted onto Pi from Youtube. You can request to playback the videos or delete them.

Stop YOUPICAST: This option is meant to kill the video running on Pi.

Youtube Share: Make sure you have exited this application before you try casting from youtube. Hit the 'TAP TO ENABLE YOUTUBE SHARE' to exit it. Once in youtube, hit share and look for 'YOUPICAST' in list of applications.
